Robert Wang, founder of Dragon House, shares his approach to evaluating new restaurant locations, asserting that operators can determine viability within two months. The article explores his research methodology, customer intelligence gathering during the initial period, and team management strategies that enable multi-site growth. By month two, novelty wears off and repeat customers reveal authentic demand patterns and operational friction points.
